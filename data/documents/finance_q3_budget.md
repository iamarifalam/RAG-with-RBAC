title: Q3 Budget Review
topic: finance
roles: finance, executive

The finance team approved a revised Q3 budget with a five percent reduction in discretionary software spend. New software requests above ten thousand dollars now require CFO approval and a written ROI note.

The July expense review also flagged duplicate subscriptions across analytics, CRM, and design teams. Consolidation should begin before the next procurement cycle, with renewals paused until owner validation is complete.
