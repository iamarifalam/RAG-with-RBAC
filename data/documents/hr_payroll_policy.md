title: Payroll Escalation Policy
topic: hr
roles: hr, executive

Payroll corrections that affect salary, bonus, or tax withholding must be reviewed by HR operations before any employee-facing response is sent. Employee bank details, salary bands, and tax identifiers are considered restricted data.

Managers can ask whether a payroll case is in progress, but they must not receive raw payroll records or personally identifiable information unless they are part of the authorized HR workflow.
