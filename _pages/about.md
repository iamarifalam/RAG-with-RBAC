    <h1>About Atlas</h1>
    <p>Atlas represents the next generation of enterprise knowledge management systems, combining cutting-edge AI technology with robust security frameworks to deliver unparalleled access to organizational intelligence.</p>
    
    <h2>Our Mission</h2>
    <p>To democratize access to enterprise knowledge while maintaining the highest standards of security, compliance, and performance. We believe that every employee should have instant access to the information they need to make informed decisions, without compromising data integrity or regulatory requirements.</p>
    
    <h2>Technical Excellence</h2>
    <p>Built by experienced engineers with deep expertise in:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>Large-scale distributed systems</li>
      <li>AI/ML model deployment and optimization</li>
      <li>Enterprise security and compliance</li>
      <li>Cloud-native application development</li>
      <li>DevOps and infrastructure automation</li>
    </ul>
    
    <h2>Security First</h2>
    <p>Security is not an afterthought—it's the foundation. Atlas implements multiple layers of protection:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>Role-based access control with domain isolation</li>
      <li>JWT-based authentication with secure token management</li>
      <li>Input validation and content filtering</li>
      <li>Audit trails and comprehensive logging</li>
      <li>Container security with minimal attack surface</li>
    </ul>
    
    <h2>Performance & Reliability</h2>
    <p>Designed for enterprise scale with:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>Sub-100ms response times</li>
      <li>99.9% uptime SLA</li>
      <li>Horizontal scaling capabilities</li>
      <li>Comprehensive monitoring and alerting</li>
      <li>Automated testing and deployment pipelines</li>
    </ul>
    
    <h2>Open Source</h2>
    <p>This project is open source to foster innovation and collaboration in the enterprise AI space. We welcome contributions from the community and are committed to maintaining high code quality and security standards.</p>
